ABOUT US

Mokgatlhane Construction & Mine Engineering Supply is a company that has invested its efforts in tailoring expert mechanisms in the sphere of Engineering as it applies to Mining and Industrial civil operations and services. We employ people with extensive knowledge and experience in the mining engineering sphere, and we are an integral service provider entirely driven by quality service delivery. Mokgatlhane Construction & Mine Engineering Supply is a black-owned and managed company with 50% of the shares belonging to women. We provide the best mining engineering solutions tailored to meet our clients' needs

Values

Mokgatlhane Construction and Mine Engineering Supply is committed to uphold the general acceptable business ethic in performing its activities. Our company maintains the following values as being of paramount importance to achieve our mission:

  • Integrity
  • Trust
  • Honesty
  • Competency
  • Dedication

Our Mission

To be the only choice, providing all clients with the highest level of service and offering an excellent service orientated approach in addressing all their Engineering, Construction, Supply and Business Solutions, with a comprehensive guarantee of satisfaction. To build relationships with our clients by gaining a thorough understanding of their business needs in assessing their current and forecasting their future requirements, thereby sharing their vision and assisting by developing and realizing the vision with them. We rate integrity first on our list of strengths.

Our Vision

To be the most reliable and efficient Engineering, Construction and Supply Service provider offering a broad spectrum of related services and products, committed to service excellence and renowned for our integrity and ability to deliver.
